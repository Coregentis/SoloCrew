# SoloCrew V1.2 Wow Moment Definition v0.1

`doc_id: SOLOCREW-V1.2-WOW-MOMENT-DEFINITION-v0.1`

## A. Purpose

This document defines the user-perceived wow moment for SoloCrew V1.2.

## B. Candidate Wow Moments

- “I can revise a packet without restarting”
- “I can compare options before deciding”
- “SoloCrew remembers my bounded preferences”
- “SoloCrew tells me what is missing before making a packet”
- “I can see why this packet is blocked or ready”

## C. Selected Wow Moment

A founder can revise a packet after insufficiency/stale evidence and see a
safer, clearer packet candidate without restarting the flow.

## D. Why This Matters

This matters because V1.1 already proves that the product can surface bounded
review posture, insufficiency, stale context, and non-executing next-step
guidance. What it does not yet give the founder is a smooth sense of momentum
after something is missing. The revision-loop wow moment makes the product feel
more helpful without crossing into live execution or control semantics.
